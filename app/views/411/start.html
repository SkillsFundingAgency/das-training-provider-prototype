Invite employers to set up an account

New employers will be sent an invitation email so they can set up and register for an apprenticeship service account.

The employer will need to enter some details, then add the PAYE scheme for their organisation and accept the legal agreement.

Before you start

You will need:

  • to have permission from the employer to use their details. Have a conversation with them to let them know to expext the invitation
  • to know the name and email address of the person you're inviting
